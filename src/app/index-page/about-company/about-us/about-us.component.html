About Us

Introduction of Ssbr iNet is a freemium educational platform website to learn coding online . Initially released in 2018, it takes its name from the Internet's World Wide Web, but is not affiliated with the Ssbr iNet consortium. Ssbr iNet works to provide courses covering all aspects of web technologies development. Ssbr iNet also publishes free HTML templates and web templates . It also has a built in online text editor called Try it Yourself. where you can edit the examples and run the code for testing. Ssbr iNet always keeps developing new courses by doing research.

Mission and Vision?

There are millions of websites created every day, also, there is much fake content spread on the internet.

So, Our main goal is to provide you with 100% Original and Safe content that provides you a great and better experience on the world wide web.

Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the digital age. We aim to be the go-to platform for high-quality online training, providing a seamless learning experience that enables our learners to achieve their goals and unlock their full potential.

Our vision is to create a global community of lifelong learners, fostering a culture of continuous skill development and innovation. We strive to bridge the gap between education and industry requirements, equipping our learners with the expertise needed to succeed in their careers and drive positive change in their respective fields.
